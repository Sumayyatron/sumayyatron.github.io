About Me

I bring creative thinking to analysis and analytic thinking to creativity.

I'm the kind of wonky nerd who makes worldbuilding spreadsheets of all her 400+ fictional characters, watches videos essays on city planning for entertainment, and thinks that the symbolic logic class required for my undergraduate Philosophy degree was "pretty fun, in retrospect!"

I'm a bit of a jack-of-all-trades in that I have worked in everything from event security to trail maintenance to clothing retail to scene painting to grantwriting to election judging, but I am determined for my next step to take me into the world of data analytics. I hope to remain there for years while continuing to write and publish speculative fiction that envisions the better world I aim to help create.

I would love to work for any San Diego-local or remote organization that uses data to better the world.
